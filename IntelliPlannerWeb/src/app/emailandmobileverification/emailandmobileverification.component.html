Teachers Registry

The Teacher Registry is a secure, centralized repository of verified educators who work in any recognized government, government-aided, private, or other schools in India. It gives each educator a single national digital identification and profile. Teachers can self-register on the platform with the help of a federated approval system. Multiple data issuers, such as schools, School Management, Education Boards, and other approved representatives, can publish teacher data on behalf of teachers, allowing multiple permitted requestors access to consented data. Teachers who register for the teacher platform gain access to a variety of interoperable and digitally-enabled services like training, transfer posting, and more.
